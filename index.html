Malaysia Energy Information Hub (MEIH) Portal
Welcome to the Malaysian Energy Information Hub (MEIH), the trusted source for comprehensive energy statistics in Malaysia. Managed by the Energy Commission (EC), we establish a centralised national energy database to support timely energy planning, provide data to stakeholders, and offer expertise in forecasting and feasibility studies. Our goal is to facilitate effective energy strategies and project implementation for a sustainable future. Join us as we drive transparency, accessibility, and collaboration in the energy sector.
